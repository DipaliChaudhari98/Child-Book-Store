Sweet with Sugar: Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 1 cup sugar + 1½ cups water (for syrup)

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, ghee, and milk into a soft dough; rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Shape into smooth balls without cracks.
  3. Fry on low-medium heat until golden brown.
  4. Mix milk powder, flour, ghee, and milk into a soft dough; rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Soak fried balls in warm syrup for 1–2 hours.
